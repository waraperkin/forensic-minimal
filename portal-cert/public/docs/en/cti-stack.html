OpenCTI connectors

Defined in docker-compose.opencti.yml:

  • MITRE ATT&CK — matrices and techniques
  • CVE — vulnerability feeds
  • OpenCTI datasets — official datasets
  • Third-party connectors: AbuseIPDB, AlienVault, MalwareBazaar, URLhaus, ThreatFox, CISA KEV, Shodan…

Activation: scripts/opencti_master_setup.py during ./forensic.sh full-start.

Typical IR workflow

  1. HELK hunting detection or ingest alert → master incident creation
  2. IOC extraction → /api/helk/export-cti or manual OpenCTI entry
  3. TheHive case creation, observables added
  4. Cortex analyzer launch (hash, URL, IP)
  5. Share relevant IOCs to MISP (community / internal)
  6. Document in portal KB + case closure

Config: config/opencti/ti-turbo.env, config/thehive/application.conf, config/cortex/application.conf. Docs: docs/PORTAL/CTI.md, docs/PORTAL/IR.md.
